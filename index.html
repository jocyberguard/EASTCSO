Welcome to EASTC Students Organization

Welcome to the official portal of the EASTC Students Organization! We are a vibrant community dedicated to enhancing student life, fostering academic excellence, and building tomorrow's leaders at the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre.

Our Mission & Objectives

  • Promote academic excellence among all students
  • Foster unity and cooperation within the student community
  • Organize social, cultural, and sporting activities
  • Represent student interests to the college administration
  • Provide platforms for leadership development
  • Support students' welfare and personal growth
